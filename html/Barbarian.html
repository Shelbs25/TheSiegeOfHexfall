The Siege of Hexfall

Rulebook & Instructions

Objective

The heroes must work together to survive the assault on Hexfall by sealing all portals and defeating the monsters that emerge. The game itself controls the monsters — players control only the heroes.

Components

  • 19 Hex Tiles (1 center, 6 inner ring, 12 outer ring)
  • Hero Miniatures (Warrior, Ranger, Mage, Barbarian)
  • Monster Miniatures (Grunts, Brutes, Boss)
  • Portal, Ruin, and Village Tokens
  • Health Rings or Trackers
  • Dice

Setup

  • Arrange the 19 hexes in a flower configuration.
  • Place exactly 3 Villages, 3 Ruins, and 3 Portals.
  • Heroes start on the center hex.
  • Set monster decks and trackers nearby.

Game Rounds

Each round consists of the following phases:

  1. Hero Phase
  2. Monster Spawn Phase
  3. Monster Action Phase
  4. End Phase

Monster Spawning

  • Before any portals are sealed, Grunts spawn from portals.
  • After the first portal is sealed, only Brutes spawn.
  • On the Boss Round, only the Boss spawns at a single portal.

Monster Attacks

  • Monsters attack in order from smallest to largest.
  • If multiple heroes occupy a hex, damage may be split between them.
  • If two heroes share a hex, they gain Hero’s Ally:
    • Heroes may combine defense.
    • Damage may be divided between heroes.

Hero Progression

  • After each round, heroes may improve one stat.
  • Heroes may heal a small amount between rounds.

Winning & Losing

Heroes win by sealing all portals and defeating all monsters. Heroes lose if all heroes are defeated.

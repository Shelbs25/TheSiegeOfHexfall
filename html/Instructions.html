The Siege of Hexfall

A cooperative heroes-vs-the-game board game

Objective

Heroes must work together to seal all Portals and defeat the Boss. If all Heroes are defeated or all Villages are destroyed, the game is lost.

Round Structure

  1. Hero Phase
  2. Monster Phase
  3. End of Round

Hero Phase

Each Hero takes a turn in any order.

  • Move: Up to 2 adjacent tiles
  • Action: Choose one
  • Attack
  • Seal a Portal
  • Loot a Ruin
  • End Turn

Monster Phase

Monster Attack Defense Health
Grunt -2 0 2
Brute 0 1 4
Boss 2 2 8

Step 1 – Spawn

  • Before any Portals are sealed, each open Portal spawns 1 Grunt.
  • Once the first Portal is sealed, Grunts stop spawning. Each open Portal now spawns 1 Brute instead.
  • On a Boss Round, only the Boss spawns at a single active Portal of the Heroes’ choice. No other monsters spawn.

Step 2 – Target Priority

  1. Adjacent Hero
  2. Nearest Hero
  3. Nearest Village

Step 3 – Move

  • Monsters move 1 tile toward their target.

Step 4 – Attack

  • Monsters attack if they are on the same tile as their target.
  • Resolve monster attacks in order from smallest to largest: Grunts → Brutes → Bosses.

Multiple Monsters & Heroes

  • If multiple Monsters are on the same tile as multiple Heroes, monsters distribute their attacks among the Heroes.
  • Heroes on the same tile may activate Hero’s Ally: Heroes may combine their Defense and split incoming damage however they choose.

Villages

Villages take 1 damage for each Monster attacking them. If all Villages are destroyed, the Heroes lose.

Victory & Defeat

  • Heroes Win: All Portals are sealed and the Boss is defeated.
  • Heroes Lose: All Heroes are defeated or all Villages are destroyed.
